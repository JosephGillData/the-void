# Senior AI Engineer Consultant At PA Consulting

[[AI/AI Infrastructure Hub|AI Infrastructure Hub]]
[[AI/Plan/Career Strategy|Career Strategy]]

## Summary

This role combines technical AI delivery with consulting, stakeholder management, and commercial awareness. It is not just about building models. It is about making AI useful, understandable, and deployable in real organisations.

## Core dimensions

- hands-on engineering
- client communication
- solution architecture
- project delivery
- pre-sales support
- internal leadership

## Why this note matters

It provides the baseline role context for the strategy in this vault. The goal is not to leave AI engineering behind, but to stretch the role toward infrastructure, security, and strategy.

## Useful implication

A strong niche in AI infrastructure can make this consulting profile more differentiated and more valuable, especially in public-sector and high-trust environments.
