## Goal

Turn private understanding into visible positioning inside and outside PA.

## Track 1: Public narrative

- publish one short post each week
- stay focused on AI infrastructure, sovereignty, and security
- explain physical and strategic realities simply

## Track 2: Internal positioning

- tell your manager where you want to specialise
- look for cloud, infrastructure, public-sector, or security-adjacent work
- write short internal notes or presentations

## Track 3: Hands-on credibility

- keep the build scope small
- use a home lab, local server, or simple ML platform experiment
- document what the practical work taught you

## Suggested themes

- where data actually lives
- what an AI data centre really looks like
- cloud versus sovereign AI
- GPUs as a strategic asset
- who controls AI infrastructure

## Success criteria

- a recognisable niche is forming
- internal stakeholders know your direction
- you have at least one concrete project or internal asset that supports the niche
